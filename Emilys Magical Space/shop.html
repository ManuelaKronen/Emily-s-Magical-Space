Birth Chart Reading

A Birth Chart Reading gets you in touch with your destiny. We are laying out the astrological map of the zodiac while interpreting the meaning of each planet with each of their given placements. Depending on your needs we will speak about your calling in life or specific questions pertaining to your current situation and potential opportunities. The reading is 90 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.

Year Ahead Transit Reading

A Year Ahead Transit Reading is a follow-up reading after I have read your birth chart. In transit reading we are looking at the upcoming astrological events and how they apply to your birth chart and life situation. Transit readings can be inspirational, problem solving, preparatory and much more. The reading is 75 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.

Healing Tree Tarot Reading

The Healing Tree Tarot reading provides you with deep insights into your past, present, and future, illuminating the issues that might hold you back and need unpacking, while directing you towards your dormant potential. You will receive an astrological add-on for free. The reading is 60 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.

Spiritual Coaching Session

The Spiritual Coaching Session is the chamaeloen of readings. You come to the session and bring up whatever lies close to your heart. It is your time to speak, your time to be heard, your time to ask away. You can ask for card draws and quick astro readings. The reading is 50 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.

Quick Fix Reading

The Quick Fix Reading is a short format reading where you can ask about anything and I will draw cards to illuminate your questions. The reading is 15 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.

Lightning Strikes Reading

The Lightning Strikes Reading is an appetiser reading where you will receive a super short and quick answer to one question. The reading is 5 minutes long and will be provided in a recorded online session.
